What Is LOZERS?

Every Episode — New Contestants.

LOZERS is a raw, unscripted online talent show — inspired by India's Got Latent — with a simple, brutal format. Every episode brings contestants who showcase their talent, and the winner is decided right in that same episode. No grand finale drama. No waiting weeks. The best one wins — right there, right then.

Any Talent Works
